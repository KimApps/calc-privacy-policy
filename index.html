Privacy Policy for Calc

Last updated: Jun 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how "Calc" ("we", "our", or "app") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Our app may collect the following information:

  • Camera access (for photo/video capture)
  • Media storage permissions (to save recordings)
  • Widget usage statistics

How We Use Information

We use this information solely to:

  • Enable camera functionality through our widget
  • Save recordings to your device storage
  • Improve app functionality
  • Store user preferences (camera facing direction, recording status)

Data Storage and Security

All photos and videos remain on your device and are not transmitted to our servers. We don't collect personal identifiable information. User preferences are stored locally using Android's SharedPreferences.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not share your information with third parties. All functionality operates locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
